How to use this messaging guide

Writing — like design — is a foundational element of the Motorola Solutions brand. It allows us to present ourselves in a consistent and unified way, regardless of audience or channel. Done correctly, it builds awareness, fosters belief and drives preference among key audiences.

This messaging guide includes the foundational elements of our brand story, as well as examples of how that story can be translated effectively for key audiences.

Note this is a guide, not a rulebook for you to repeat verbatim, but a messaging framework for you to interpret and communicate, as needed through your own lens.

This guide will help you:
  • +understand the basic strategic framing that sets up our brand messaging approach
  • +understand the core elements of the Motorola Solutions brand positioning and deliver against it
  • +present Motorola Solutions to the market in a consistent and unified way
  • +tailor messaging to specific stakeholder audiences
  • +explore new ways to put the Motorola Solutions story and voice to work

Brand Narrative: Do's and Don'ts

It's important to apply "Solving for safer" correctly, to ensure we consistently and effectively communicate what we do. An incorrect application of our brand narrative can lead to brand confusion and dilute the meaning of what we do and the impact we make.

Do

Keep the integrity of the lock-up: [Solving for safer]
Solving for safer
Capitalize 'Safer' when referencing our narrative as a proper noun
Learn more about our Safer Schools solutions
Use capitalization only when referencing "Safer X" as a proper noun
Safer communities. Safer schools. Safer hospitals. Safer stadiums. Safer businesses. Safer everywhere.
Please note that the industries / places should NOT appear in the lock-up (i.e. "Solving for safer x" is NOT acceptable.
(When highlighting the verticals we serve as a list - do not change the order in which our industries appear)
Safer communities. Safer schools. Safer hospitals. Safer stadiums. Safer businesses. Safer everywhere.

Do not

Replace or omit words from the lockup
We are solving for better
Solving for tomorrow
Solving for a better, safer world
Volunteering for safer
Imply that our work is done
We solved for safer
We solved for a safe world
We are solving for safe
Dilute the narrative
[Product] is solving for safer
Change the order the words appear
Safer for solving
Capitalize "s" in Safer
Solving For Safer
Solving for Safer
Add industries / places into the lockup. The lock-up must remain independent.
Solving for safer communities
Solving for safer schools
Solving for safer hospitals
Solving for safer stadiums

Narrative Messaging: Stump Speech (Inspiration)

At Motorola Solutions we help make everywhere safer.

Why do I say that? Because every day we come to work to help create safer communities, safer schools, safer hospitals, safer businesses, safer everywhere. We do it in two key ways:

First, we build the best possible technology we can across every part of the safety and security ecosystem to help protect people, property and places. That's mission-critical communication devices and networks that perform in the harshest conditions and are proven to help you stay connected. It's video security powered by responsibly-built AI that can alert you when action is needed, focusing human attention to act with more certainty. And it's the ability to unite voice, video and data in a single command center view, connecting the dots to make decision-making faster and more accurate. So that in an active shooter situation, we can highlight irregularities early, identify the threat, shut down access and alert those who need to know.

Second, we connect public safety agencies and enterprises, enabling the collaboration that's critical for a more proactive approach to safety and security. We're known for our work on the front lines supporting police, fire and other emergency responders. And with our rapidly growing base of private businesses using the same technologies – schools, hospitals and stadiums - we support the intersection of public, private and people, connecting those in need with those who can help. Think about this: in the U.S., as many as 10,000 lives could be saved every year by reducing 911 response times by just one minute.

It's not only about what we do but how we do it. Grounded in nearly 100 years of close customer and community collaboration, we design and advance technology that empower human decision-makers at the center – intentionally, purposefully, responsibly. We're driven by being a positive force for change and our commitment to doing the right thing to help make everywhere safer for us all.

Narrative Messaging: About Us

At Motorola Solutions we help make everywhere safer.

We build and connect safety and security technologies in a never-ending pursuit to strengthen safety everywhere. Individually, our solutions enable public safety agencies and enterprises to help protect people, property and places. Connected, they enable the collaboration that's critical for a proactive approach to safety and security.

Our innovation in public safety began nearly a century ago with the advent of mission-critical communications radios and networks, and spans the first car radio, to the communications on the Apollo missions to the radios trusted as a lifeline by emergency responders all over the world today. In the many decades since our founding, we have purposefully transformed our business beyond radio to build an ecosystem of technologies centered on helping to keep people safe and secure everywhere.

Today, we make critical communications devices and networks that perform in the harshest conditions and are proven to help teams stay connected and communicate with clarity. We design video security systems powered by responsibly-built AI that can alert people when action is needed, focusing human attention to cover more ground and act with more certainty. We unify voice, video and data feeds into the command center, connecting the dots to simplify workflows and help provide the overarching perspective to make decisions with greater focus, accuracy and speed.

Motorola Solutions is driven by a belief that safety is a shared responsibility, built upon trust, transparency and collaboration. We advance our innovations both by anticipating the needs and inviting the involvement of our customers and communities. We design technologies that empower human decision-makers at the center – intentionally, purposefully, responsibly. We're driven by being a positive force for change and our commitment to doing the right thing to help make everywhere safer for us all.

Learn more about how we're helping create safer communities, safer schools, safer hospitals, safer businesses, safer everywhere at www.motorolasolutions.com.
